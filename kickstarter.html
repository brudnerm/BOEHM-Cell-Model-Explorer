Help Us Expand Our Collection of Cancer Cell Models

The Broad Institute’s Cancer Cell Line Factory (CCLF) initiative aims to generate and characterize immortalized cell models isolated from patients’ tumors and make them available to the scientific community. These models are added to the Cancer Cell Line Encyclopedia (CCLE) collection. As the collection grows, there is an increasing need to understand the overall distribution of these cell models and how well they represent the diversity of cancer. Cancer Cell Line Explorer (CCLX) allows users to visualize and find cell models with specific attributes in the CCLE/CCLF collection.
